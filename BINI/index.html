Bini (stylized as BINI) is an eight-member Filipino girl group formed by ABS-CBN Star Hunt Academy, consisting of Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena. Their pre-debut single "Da Coconut Nut" was released on November 20, 2020. The group officially debuted on June 11, 2021 with the release of their debut single Born To Win.
